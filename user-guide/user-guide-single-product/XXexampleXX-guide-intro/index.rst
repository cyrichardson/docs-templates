=====================
How to use this guide
=====================
We created this guide to help you get the most from XXexampleXX
and especially from its key features:

* something innovative

* something amazing

* something important

* something unique

The guide is meant to simplify your startup process.
It doesn't replace the detailed technical documentation for these
services, but it should give you a clear picture of
what is available to you as a Rackspace customer.

* :ref:`XXexampleXX-intro`
  provides a high-level overview of XXexampleXX,
  explains how XXexampleXX differs from competing products,
  shows how XXexampleXX relates to other Rackspace products,
  and connects you to our collection of user stories.

* :ref:`XXexampleXX-interfaces` suggests several ways of
  controlling your configuration, including the GUI-based Cloud Control
  Panel and several CLIs, APIs, and SDKs.

* :ref:`XXexampleXX-config`, focuses on the details of XXexampleXX concepts and
  avalilable actions, providing facts and suggestions to help you quickly
  establish a useful and manageable XXexampleXX configuration.

* :ref:`XXexampleXX-preprod` specifies pre-production steps you should
  take to harden your configuration and make it ready for work.

* :ref:`XXexampleXX-ops` identifies ongoing
  responsibilities and suggests special-interest topics that may
  interest you after you are established in the XXexampleXX.
